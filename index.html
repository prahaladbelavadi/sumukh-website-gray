About

Hi! I am a law graduate pursuing my masters in Security and Defence Laws from NALSAR.
I work on public policy and aspire to bring about a fundamental change in the way policy is designed and implemented.

I draw my inspiration from a statement of Raja Rantideva:
न त्वहं कामये राज्यं न स्वर्गं नापुनर्भवम् । कामये दुःखतप्तानां प्रणिनां आर्तिनाशनम् ॥
"I desire neither for crown nor for heaven nor even for salvation; the only desire I cherish is the eradication of sorrows and suffering of all living creatures."

When I am not working on policy issues, I ride my Himalayan, Climb Rocks and try food at new places. I also consult for businesses to help them to set up processes, acquire clients and resolve conflicts. I rarely write. I am working on being more consistent in that. You can find my thoughts in the blog section.

A mentor once told me, "speak only if you can make the conversation better". So I stick to it most of the times.

I come with a lot of flaws which I am working to fix. If you know me and you feel I need to improve on anything, please leave feedback in the box below. I always strive to become a better version of myself and your feedback will help me.

I guess that's it for now. You can follow me on social media with the links below.
